How It Works

Simple, transparent, and completely private.

Organize Your Digital Life

RevVault isn't just a place to store passwords; it's a secure digital vault for your entire online identity. Keep your login credentials, sensitive notes, and security questions neatly organized and easily accessible.

With our intuitive dashboard, you can instantly find what you need, securely copy passwords to your clipboard, and identify weak or reused passwords before they become a liability.

🗄️

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

We believe your data belongs to you. That's why RevVault is built on a strict zero-knowledge architecture. Your master password is never transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Your vault is encrypted locally on your device using PBKDF2 key derivation and AES-256-GCM authenticated encryption. This means that even in the unlikely event of a server breach, your data remains mathematically impossible to access without your master password.

🔐

Advanced Account Protection

Security is layered. Beyond your master password, RevVault supports comprehensive Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) via time-based one-time passwords (TOTP).

Pair your account with an authenticator app like Google Authenticator or Authy to ensure that even if someone learns your master password, they still cannot access your secure vault.
